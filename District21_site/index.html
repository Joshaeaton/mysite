About us

District 21 is a refined evening dining experience located right on the Francis Tuttle Rockwell campus. We are focused on bringing the best of the culinary world to your dinner table. Our highly skilled chefs have trained day in day out to create only the best gourmet creations from around the world just for you. So grab a reservation and prepare to have your taste buds taken on a journey.
